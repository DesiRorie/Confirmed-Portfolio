My portfolio

Bounty Hunter

Is a mobile game that lets the player choose out of 3 paths to determine the game play. The menu consists of a Shop, Bag, Gold, and Player button. The shop allows the player to purchase weapons and health with the starting amount of gold given. When ready to fight the boss the player can equip the weapon and press fight. If the players health gets too low they can use an health item mid-fight. MVVM was the pattern of choice for this app.
